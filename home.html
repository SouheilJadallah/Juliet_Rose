About us

At Juliet Rose Jewellers, every piece tells a story—yours. Rooted in generations of craftsmanship, owner Sam carries forward a proud family legacy, blending timeless tradition with inspired creativity. From a young age, he was immersed in the art of fine jewelry, learning not just the techniques, but the meaning behind every stone and setting. Today, Sam is known for his exceptional ability to listen deeply and design with heart. Whether he’s redesigning wedding bands, resizing a cherished heirloom, or crafting a custom piece from sentimental materials, his work is defined by precision, passion, and personal connection. Specializing in custom design, jewelry repair, and watch services, Juliet Rose is where old treasures are reimagined and new memories are made. Our clients consistently praise Sam for his meticulous attention to detail and genuine care—making each visit a meaningful experience. Let us help you tell your story, beautifully.
